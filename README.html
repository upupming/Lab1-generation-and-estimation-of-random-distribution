定义高斯混合分布：

(11)pM(X)=∑i=1kαip(X∣μi,Σi)p(X∣μi,Σi)=exp{−12(X−μi)TΣi−1(X−μi)}(2π)D/2∣Σi∣12 \begin{aligned} p_M(\mathrm{\mathbf{X}}) &= \sum_{i = 1}^k \alpha_i p(\mathrm{\mathbf{X}}|\mathrm{\mathbf{\mu _i}}, \mathrm{\mathbf{\Sigma _i}}) \\ p(\mathrm{\mathbf{X}}|\mathrm{\mathbf{\mu _i}}, \mathrm{\mathbf{\Sigma _i}}) &= \frac{exp\{-\frac{1}{2}(\mathrm{\mathbf{X}} - \mathrm{\mathbf{\mu_i}})^T\mathrm{\mathbf{\Sigma_i^{-1}}}(\mathrm{\mathbf{X}} - \mathrm{\mathbf{\mu_i}})\}}{(2\pi) ^ {D/2}|\Sigma_i|^{\frac{1}{2}}} \end{aligned} \tag{11} pM​(X)p(X∣μi​,Σi​)​=i=1∑k​αi​p(X∣μi​,Σi​)=(2π)D/2∣Σi​∣21​exp{−21​(X−μi​)TΣi−1​(X−μi​)}​​(11)
